### Question 2

The solution set of \((2x-3)(x+5)\le 0\) is:

- **A.** \((-\infty,-5)\cup\left(\frac{3}{2},\infty\right)\) (typical wrong form)  
- **B.** …  
- **C.** …  
- **D.** \([-5,\frac{3}{2}]\) (correct closed interval between roots; confirm option letters)

**Solution.**  
Roots: \(x=-5\) and \(x=\frac{3}{2}\). Open-up parabola; product \(\le 0\) between roots:  
\[
x \in \left[-5,\frac{3}{2}\right].
\]  
Pick the option that matches this interval.

### Question 4

A **20 m** building casts a shadow **4 m**. At the same time, a **35 m** tree casts a shadow **\(x\)** m. Find **\(x\)**.

- **A.** 8 m  
- **B.** 7 m  
- **C.** 15 m  
- **D.** …  

**Solution.**  
Similar triangles: \(\dfrac{20}{4}=\dfrac{35}{x}\Rightarrow 5=\dfrac{35}{x}\Rightarrow x=7\).  

**Answer: B** (if B is 7 m)

### Question 5

A car goes **9 km East**, then **4 km North**, then **12 km West**. How far is it from the start?

- **A.** 25 km  
- **B.** 12 km  
- **C.** 4 km  
- **D.** 5 km  

**Solution.**  
Net displacement: \((9-12,\,4)=(-3,4)\) km. Distance \(=\sqrt{9+16}=5\) km.  

**Answer: D**

About Ethiopian Grade 12 Exams

Welcome to Grade 12 Exam Practice Platform

This platform provides comprehensive practice materials for Ethiopian Grade 12 national entrance and remedial examinations. Students can access previous exam questions with detailed solutions to enhance their preparation.

Features

  • Entrance Exams: Practice previous national entrance examination questions
  • Remedial Exams: Access remedial examination materials
  • Multiple Years: Questions from 2015, 2016, 2017 and more
  • Natural & Social Streams: Comprehensive coverage of all subjects
  • Detailed Solutions: Step-by-step explanations for every question
  • Math Support: Full mathematical notation support for Mathematics and Physics

How to Use

  1. Select your exam type (Entrance or Remedial)
  2. Choose the year
  3. Pick your stream (Natural or Social)
  4. Select the course/subject
  5. Start practicing and check solutions
